Oven-baked tomato feta pasta

Description

Tik-Tok famous italian pasta with oven-baked feta cheese and tomatos.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Feta cheese
  • Bell pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Tomatos
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Set the oven on 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Cut the bell pepper, garlic, onion into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Fill an oven form with the ingredients, doused in the oil.
  4. Let bake for 25-40 minutes, until the vegetables gets some color.
  5. Boil your pasta of choice, once the vegetables are out mix them up with a spoon, crushing the tomatoes carefully. Mix in the pasta and serve.
