Tacos

Description

Soft taco shells with fillings.

Ingredients

  • Soft tortilla shells
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Cucumber
  • Shredded cheese
  • Creme fraiche
  • Bell pepper
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into good sizes.
  2. Panfry the tortilla in oil until golden, but not too stiff.
  3. Serve.
